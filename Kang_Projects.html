Projects

Project Name: Compound Machine; Tennis Ball K-9 Cannon

Course: Principles of Engineering

Project Duration: 8/31/17 - 9/9/17

Project Type: Group

Objective: Design, Build, and Test a Prototype of a Machine that can Complete an Everyday Task

The objective of this project, was to construct a compound machine that completes an everyday task. In this project, groups had to turn in a single document that contained their design proposal, modifications, and final design. Furthermore, the compound machine needed to utilize four simple machines and must be powered by a single human input. This all had to be done in six days, and after brainstorming, me and my group decided to create a ball throwing machine through the use of a high gear ratio compound train. I was the project manager for my group, and I lead the team in the documentation of our project, doing most of the documentation, recording all of the modifications made, and writing the final design descriptions. I also worked as the assistant builder proposed numerous ideas in order to make the tennis ball shooter better.

From this project, I learned how to document any modifications made during the project, and how to construct a formal design brief. I also learned more about the importance of reducing friction within mechanisms, and methods to reduce friction within mechanisms. This project exemplified my ability to lead a project, and create a working environment that allowed my group to complete the project effectively, and finish earlier than the deadline.
