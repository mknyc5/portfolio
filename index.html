Personal Life

The moment I stepped into Dublin High, I promised to myself that I would make the most out of my high school career. Every year, I sought new things to try, anything that would get me out of my comfort zone.

I look to be the first in my family to pursue an engineering career in hopes to better our society. My ultimate goal is to become financially stable through an engineering career, and then be able to pursue a career in teaching and coaching.

Elephant in the Room

Sparked by the suicide of a friend, I created Elephant in the Room in hopes to spark open mindedness, and provide DHS students with the tools they may need to cope with stress, depression, or suicidal thoughts. Through public speaking events called gatherings we provide students a safe place to speak about their stories, and encourage open discourse.
